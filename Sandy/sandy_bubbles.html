About this graphic: Each bubble represents roughly 2,000 people. Data for this visualization was based on the New York City Community Development Block Grant - Disaster Recovery Partial Action Plan A, which drew on FEMA data to identify the Hurricane Sandy Operational Inundation Area. The report cited 2010 Census data and the 2009 - 2011 American Community Survey (ASC) data for demographic trends.
